Easily create, delete, and manage factions更容易创建，删除和保护公会
Players in the same faction don't inflict PVP damage on each other同一个公会的人在pvp里不会伤害对方
Kick annoying players把骚扰别人的玩家踢掉
Invite anyone you want (they can accept or deny the invite)能邀请所有玩家加入公会（他们也可以选择同意或拒绝
Claim plots and create a dominating base领取一个地皮和创建一个捐款箱（要经济核心支持）
Three ranks: Member, Officer and Leader三个公会竞争排名：人数，工作人员，领导者
Commands指令

/f about - view plugin information查看公会信息
/f accept - accept an invite接收邀请
/f claim - claim a plot of land (two snow blocks will mark out the corners)领取一块公会地皮
/f create - create a faction创建一个公会
/f del - delete your faction (if you are the leader)删除公会
/f demote <player> - demote a player from Officer to Member提拔一个玩家
/f deny - decline an invite拒绝邀请
/f home - go to your Faction's home回到公会设置的加
/f help - display all FactionsPro commands关于公会插件指令
/f invite <player> - invite a player邀请一个玩家加入

/f kick <player> - remove someone from the faction踢掉一个公会成员
/f leader <player> - give your leadership to someone else给会长权利别人
/f leave - leave a faction you are currently in离开一个公会
/f promote <player> - promote a player to Officer rank提升一个玩家排名
/f sethome - set your Faction's home point设置公会共同家的地点
/f unclaim - unclaim your plot删除公会地皮
/f unsethome unset your Faction's home删除之前设置的家
